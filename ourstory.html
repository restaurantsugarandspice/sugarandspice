Our Story

Sugar and Spice was born out of our love for delectable desserts and great cheer. All our desserts are hand-made daily and we guarantee the freshest of ingredients. Our greatest wish is for our customers to leave with a smile on their face. As the old adage says, "Sugar and Spice Makes Everything Nice." With every morsel, we hope our customers experience the nicer side of life.
